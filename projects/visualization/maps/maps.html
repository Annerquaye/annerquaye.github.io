Recreating maps using QGIS, ai2HTML

By Richard A. Abbey

I used QGIS to recreate some maps from the New York Times on the covid-19 case count among colleges and universities


I used Google Earth, Adobe Illustrator and ai2HTML to recreate one of these maps from New York Times. This map illustrates how people were fleeing the Ukranian city of Irpin.


I used Google Earth and Illustrator to create a map that gives context this Chicago Times story about the expansion of the Asian population in Chicago. I believe without a map, any reader unfamiliar with Chicago would find it hard to picture how this expansion is playing out.
