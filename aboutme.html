About me

Richard A. Abbey is a Ghanaian award-winning business journalist and an Msc Data Journalism candidate at Columbia University’s Graduate School of Journalism. He's worked as a journalist in the last decade in his home country, Ghana where he rose to the level of Managing Editor in his last role at the country's first-ever digital business publication, Business24. He's passionate about using data to tell stories of underserved and marginalized communities. He's covered events such as the World Bank/IMF meetings, Mobile World Congress etc. He likes to see new places.
